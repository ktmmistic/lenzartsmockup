Museum-Quality Custom Framing

Preserve and showcase your treasured artwork, photographs, and memorabilia with our expert custom framing services. Over 55 years of experience protecting what matters most to you.

Proudly serving Santa Cruz since 1968

Conservation Framing

Museum-quality, archival framing using acid-free materials and UV-protective glazing to ensure your artwork survives for generations. Perfect for valuable art, family photos, and important documents.

  • Archival matting and backing
  • UV-filtering glazing options
  • Reversible mounting techniques
  • Climate-controlled environment

Custom Matting

Professional mat cutting and design to perfectly complement your artwork. Choose from hundreds of colors and textures, with options for multiple openings and decorative techniques.

  • Premium mat board selection
  • Multiple opening designs
  • Decorative mat techniques
  • Color matching consultation

Frame Selection

Extensive collection of frame styles from traditional to contemporary. Wood, metal, and specialty frames in every size and finish to match your decor and personal style.

  • Hundreds of frame styles
  • Custom sizing available
  • Traditional and modern options
  • Expert style consultation

Specialty Projects

From oversized artwork to three-dimensional objects, we handle unique framing challenges with creative solutions and expert craftsmanship.

  • Oversized artwork framing
  • Shadow boxes and display cases
  • Sports memorabilia framing
  • Wedding and memorial displays
