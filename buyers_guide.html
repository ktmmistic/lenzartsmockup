Our monthly Buyer's Guides open the world of art to you! Hundreds of sale items are listed for your perusal. While many discounted products are published, there are many unadvertised in-store specials you'll discover when you visit Lenz Arts in person!

Where to Find Our Guide

Primary Publication: Buyer's Guides are inserted in The Santa Cruz Sentinel, usually the Monday closest to the 1st of every month, though if there's a holiday conflict, we may move it a week earlier or later.

Additional Publications: We also occasionally insert into the Scotts Valley Banner and The Valley Times.
