Privacy Statement

Your privacy is important to us. Any information you provide is used solely by Lenz Arts, Inc. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information—including your email address—with third parties, except as required by law or when necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Lenz Arts, Inc., our employees, or the public.

If you have questions about our privacy practices, please contact us.
